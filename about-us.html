Who We Are?

About Us

Fillam Fillam is video Advertising agency and Production Company based in Rajkot, Est. in 2013, Fillam Fillam makes Advertisements, Corporate Films, Short & Feature Films, Music Videos, Web Series/Show, TV Serial etc. Fillam Fillam has a pool of highly creative, trained and motivated professionals working towards creating unique and creative contents for their clients.

Fillam Fillam is an idea to cater the professional services to movie buffs, film-makers and to those who value the medium of audio-visual for their commercial and corporate branding.

Fillam Fillam understands the value of Creativity/Originality along with the technical know-how to execute an audio-visual project. It is the company having all the film equipments, experienced and passionate team to fulfill the requirements of production houses, independent filmmakers, media agencies as well as the corporate who want to design and create their visual branding. Fillam Fillam also gives its services to NGO for their awareness programs and for the Government agencies for their audio-visual aid.

At Fillam Fillam, we know that success of our business is directly related to the success of our clients. We listen carefully and respond quickly to the needs of our clients and constantly expand our services and deliver high quality products.
