About Us

Fillam Fillam is video Advertising agency and Production Company based in Rajkot, Est. in 2013, Fillam Fillam makes Advertisements, Corporate Films, Short & Feature Films, Music Videos, Web Series/Show, TV Serial etc. Fillam Fillam has a pool of highly creative, trained and motivated professionals working towards creating unique and creative contents for their clients.
